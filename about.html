Background Of Gorkha Group

Background:

Gorkha International Public Secondary School was founded in 1998 A.D. by a group of Gorkhas(Lahures) and was inaurgurated by the Vice Chancellor of kathamndu University, Professor Dr. Suresh Raj Sharma on the 14th of chaitra 2055 B.S. The school has been promotes by Gorkha Group Private Limited Dang. Gorkha Group Dang has been executing Gorkha International Public Secondary School Ghorahi, Gorkha Secondary Shool Tulsipur, Gorkha Model Secondary School Lamahi, Gorkha Ideal School Lalmatiya, Victoria International college Ghorahi, Gorkha Model College Tulsipur, Swornim college Lamahi and Gorkha Public Hospital Ghorahi.



Academic Achivement:

The school has been able to achieve a very good result in see, class 11 and class 12 board examination since its first batch. Our students have sucessfully completed technical education, getting scholarships in Medicine, Engineering, Agriculture, Information Technology, Management streams. They have also been to find excellent jobs within the country and inforeign countries.
